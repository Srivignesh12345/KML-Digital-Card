History of Kalpana Microbiology Lab

Kalpana Microbiology Lab was established with a strong commitment to providing accurate, reliable, and ethical diagnostic services to the community. From its inception, the laboratory has focused exclusively on pathology and microbiology, ensuring high standards of scientific precision and patient care.

Under the leadership of Mr. M. Sadhasivam, the laboratory has continuously evolved by adopting standardized laboratory practices, advanced methodologies, and strict quality control measures.

Over the years, Kalpana Microbiology Lab has earned the trust of clinicians and patients alike by delivering consistent results, timely reports, and dependable diagnostic support across a wide range of investigations.
